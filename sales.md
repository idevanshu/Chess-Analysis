<h2>Chess Legends — The Ultimate Chess Experience</h2>

<p>
Chess Legends is not just another chess app. It's a full-featured, AI-powered chess platform that combines the thrill of competitive play with cutting-edge technology, real-time multiplayer, and entertainment — all in one place.
</p>

---

<h2>Why Chess Legends?</h2>

<p>
Whether you're a beginner looking to learn or a seasoned player chasing your peak rating, Chess Legends adapts to you. Play against world-class AI personalities, challenge friends in real-time, and track every aspect of your improvement with deep analytics — all from your browser.
</p>

---

<h2>Play Against Legendary AI Opponents</h2>

<p>
Face off against 6 iconic AI personalities — each modeled after real-world chess legends like Magnus Carlsen, Garry Kasparov, Hikaru Nakamura, Praggnanandhaa, Bobby Fischer, and Judit Polgar. Every opponent has a unique playing style, ELO rating (1320–3190), and personality that makes each match feel different.
</p>

<p>
Under the hood, Stockfish 18 powers every AI move — the strongest open-source chess engine in the world. Difficulty scales dynamically based on the opponent you choose, so you always get a fair fight.
</p>

---

<h2>Real-Time Online Multiplayer</h2>

<p>
Create a room, share the code, and play anyone — anywhere. Our WebSocket-powered multiplayer delivers instant move synchronization, live opponent status, in-game chat, draw offers, takeback requests, rematch creation, and automatic reconnection if you drop.
</p>

<p>
Choose from 10+ time controls — Bullet (1+0, 2+1), Blitz (3+0, 5+3), Rapid (10+0, 15+10), Classical (30+0, 30+20), or Unlimited. Server-side clock tracking ensures fair play across all formats.
</p>

---

<h2>Live AI Commentary — "Gary the Grand Commentator"</h2>

<p>
Every match gets the broadcast treatment. Our AI commentator reacts to your moves in real time — hyping up brilliant plays, roasting blunders, and narrating the game like a WWE announcer who happens to be a Grandmaster. It's chess entertainment you won't find anywhere else.
</p>

<p>
Powered by GPT, the commentary is streamed live as you play — punchy, hilarious, and always in character. Toggle it on or off anytime.
</p>

---

<h2>Deep Game Analysis</h2>

<p>
After every game, get a full breakdown of your performance. Each move is classified — Best, Excellent, Good, Inaccuracy, Mistake, or Blunder — with centipawn loss calculations, accuracy scores, and an evaluation bar that shows who's winning at every point.
</p>

<p>
Opening detection identifies the ECO code and name of your opening. Key moments are highlighted so you can study where the game turned. Navigate through your entire move history with a single click.
</p>

---

<h2>Comprehensive Dashboard & Analytics</h2>

<p>
Your dashboard is your chess command center. Track your ELO rating, win rate, win/loss streaks, move accuracy trends, and per-opponent performance — all updated in real time with interactive charts and graphs.
</p>

<p>
See how you perform as White vs Black, which openings give you the best results, and how your accuracy trends over your last games. Every stat you need to improve is right there.
</p>

---

<h2>Sound, Feel & Premium UI</h2>

<p>
Custom-crafted wood-click sound effects, capture thuds, check alerts, and game-end chords — all synthesized in-browser for zero latency. The board uses a warm walnut-and-cream color scheme with smooth drag-and-drop, premove support, promotion chooser, hint arrows, and a responsive layout that works beautifully on both desktop and mobile.
</p>

---

<h2>Built for Scale</h2>

<p>
The backend runs on Express + Socket.IO with MongoDB for persistent storage, JWT authentication for security, and Prometheus metrics for production monitoring. Health checks, readiness probes, and structured data models (User, Game, GameRoom, Performance) make Chess Legends ready for deployment at any scale.
</p>

<h2>What's Coming Next — The Future of Chess Legends</h2>

<p>
We're just getting started. Here's what's on the roadmap:
</p>

<h2>Train Your Own AI</h2>

<p>
Imagine building your own chess AI — trained on your style, your openings, your tendencies. Upload your games, fine-tune the engine, and create a digital version of yourself that plays the way you play. Challenge friends to beat <strong>your</strong> AI, or pit it against other player-trained models. Your chess brain, available 24/7.
</p>

<h2>Make Your AI Public</h2>

<p>
Once your AI is trained, publish it to the Chess Legends AI marketplace. Let players around the world challenge your creation. Climb the leaderboard not just as a player — but as an AI trainer. Build a reputation for creating the toughest, most creative, or most unpredictable AI opponents on the platform.
</p>

<h2>Train a Weaker AI & Learn From It</h2>

<p>
Not every training exercise needs a Grandmaster. Create a deliberately weaker AI — one that makes the kind of mistakes you're trying to learn to punish. Train a model that plays like a 1200-rated player who always falls for the same traps, or one that avoids your favorite openings so you're forced to adapt. Use it as a personal sparring partner that's built to make you better.
</p>

<h2>Learn From Someone Else's AI</h2>

<p>
Play against AIs trained by stronger players and absorb their patterns. If a 2200-rated player publishes their AI, you can study how it handles positions, which openings it favors, and where it punishes mistakes. It's like having a training partner from any skill level, available anytime — without needing them to be online.
</p>
